FANBOYS FIELD GUIDE (for, and, nor, but, or, yet, so)

COMPOUND SENTENCE: two subjects, each with its own verb, joined by a FANBOYS word → comma before it.
Ex: The rain stopped, so we went outside.

COMPOUND PREDICATE: one subject doing two things, joined by a FANBOYS word → no comma.
Ex: The dog barked and chased its tail.
